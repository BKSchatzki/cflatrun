Activities

Community

Carnegie Mellon Spring Festival: C Flat Run is excited to be performing at the Carnegie Mellon Spring Festival, an annual event that celebrates the arts and culture of Pittsburgh. We'll be taking the stage with other local performers to share our love of a cappella music and connect with our community. Join us for a day of great music, food, and fun!

Pittsburgh Pride Parade: C Flat Run is proud to participate in the Pittsburgh Pride Parade, an annual event that celebrates the LGBTQ+ community and promotes equality and inclusivity for all. We believe that music can bring people together and inspire positive change, and we're honored to be part of such an important event. Come out and join us as we sing and march in solidarity with our community!

Competitions

Acapalooza: C Flat Run is excited to compete in Acapalooza, a fun and friendly competition between a cappella groups from different schools and universities. This event provides a great opportunity for us to showcase our talents, connect with other singers, and have some fun. We hope you'll come out and support us as we compete for top honors!

ICCA Competition: C Flat Run is thrilled to participate in the International Championship of Collegiate A Cappella (ICCA) competition series. This prestigious competition brings together the best a cappella groups from around the world to compete for national recognition. We're excited to show off our skills, learn from other talented groups, and have a chance to advance to the national level. We hope you'll come out and support us as we take the stage at the ICCA!

Fundraisers

Singing for a Cause: C Flat Run is proud to participate in Singing for a Cause, an annual benefit concert that supports local charities and organizations. This event provides a platform for us to use our music to raise awareness and make a positive impact on our community. We invite everyone to come out and support a great cause while enjoying some great a cappella music.

Voices for Change: C Flat Run is excited to perform at Voices for Change, a music festival that celebrates diversity and promotes social and political awareness. We believe that music can be a powerful tool for change, and we're honored to be part of an event that brings together artists, activists, and thought leaders to share their voices and inspire change. Join us for a day of great music and meaningful dialogue.
